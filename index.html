Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the event, access, and recordings.

Will recordings be available after the live event?

Yes! All ticket holders receive full access to session recordings and speaker slides within 24 hours after the live broadcast finishes.

Who is this event designed for?

This event is built for software engineers, product designers, and tech leaders looking to scale their skills and build modern web applications.

Can I transfer my ticket to a colleague?

Absolutely. You can transfer your pass up to 48 hours before the event starts through your account portal or by contacting support.

Are there team or bulk discounts available?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount for groups of 5 or more. Select the "Team Pass" tier at checkout or reach out to our sales team for custom invoicing.

What tools or prerequisites do I need to join?

No special software is required—just a web browser and a stable internet connection. Workshop files will be provided via GitHub repositories prior to the event.

What is your refund policy?

We offer full refunds up to 7 days before the event date. After that window, tickets can still be transferred to another person or converted into a credit for future events.
